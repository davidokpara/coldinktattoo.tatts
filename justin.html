JUSTIN

I was born and raised in Los Angeles, California. I have always found myself interested in art. I have been drawing since I was 4 years old and I would try to create art out of anything that I could get my hands on. The thing I love about art so much is the freedom of creativity. I appreciate the joy that comes with giving someone a tattoo and seeing that smile that hits home once it is finished. I have been tattooing professionally for 3 years and I generally like to do all styles of art from small pieces to large pieces, color animation, black and grey, realism and many more. I am always up for a challenge. I pride myself on professionalism, my ability to expand my knowledge in tattooing, and bringing your vision into a reality. So, what are you waiting for? Please send me your dope ideas and let's create that tattoo you have always wanted.
